THOR: GOD OF THUNDER

>

FAMILY

Thor is the son of Odin, chief of the Æsir, and Jörð, the living personification of the Earth.
He is the eldest of Odin's sons and is the half-brother of Baldr, Höðr, Víðarr, and Váli.
He is married to the golden-haired goddess Sif, with whom he has a duaghter, Þrúðr.
Thor has a jötunn lover Járnsaxa, with whom he has two sons, Móði and Magni.
