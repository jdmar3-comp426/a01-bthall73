THOR: GOD OF THUNDER

>

POPULAR CULTURE

Thor is a character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, his full name being Thor Odinson, son of Odin and Frigga.
He is the god of thunder and one of the founding members of the Avengers.
The Australian actor Chris Hemsworth plays the role of Thor, being featured in Thor (2011), The Avengers (2012),
Thor: The Dark World (2013), Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015), Thor: Ragnarok (2017), Avengers: Infinity War (2018), and Avengers: Endgame (2019).
